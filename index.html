App Features

Blue Pail makes sure that your plants will never go dry again with custom reminders scheduled for the times that are most convenient for you.

Simple

Set up and manage your plants with a simple to use interface where no ads or account sign-in's will get in your way.

Organized

Group and filter your plants with custom tags, and track your plant's watering schedule by entering in the amount of days to the next watering and the time you want to be notified on that day. Adding a photo for you plant will also help to identify it in the app.

Reliable

Get reminders that are always based on your schedule: track your plant watering schedules by entering in the amount of days to the next watering and the time you want to be notifited.
